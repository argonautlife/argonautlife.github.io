COVID-19 Medical Protective Equipment: Non-Surgical Isolation Gown

MODA3000 is a ANSI/AAMI Level 1 disposable personal protective equipment suitable for healthcare patients and personnel who are currently working in the frontline of combat against COVID-19. MODA3000 is designed to prevent the penetration of microorganisms, body fluids and particulate material and is certified by FDA, CE, and EN 14126.
