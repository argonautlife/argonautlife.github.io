COVID-19 Medical Protective Equipment

MODA3000 is a Type 4, level D personal protective equipment suitable for healthcare workers and government officials who are currently working in the frontline of combat against COVID-19. MODA3000 is designed specifically for COVID-19 to prevent the penetration of microbial blood chemicals and has passed testing by the Korea Apparel Testing Research Institute (KATRAI) and FITI, an internationally accredited testing accreditation body.

Components

1 set of protective clothing/suit +
1 pair of shoe covers
